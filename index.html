Welcome to Mukilan university

Mukilan University Counselling 2017 Vacancy Position Live is updated from the official source at the Vacancy Position Sub-Domain. You shall check the vacancy position after every counselling session. The remaining seats available in the colleges and branches will be updated community wise. You shall make the query and finalise your list of choices for your counselling. We advice students to check the vacancy position status before attending the counselling. The vacancy position status will be displayed in the Mukilan University campus in LED displays during the counselling session. You shall use them if you do not have internet connection by the time.
